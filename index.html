CSS Shootout

Taking a break from the norm to get you thinking outside the box

  • You will be presented with 4 problems of increasing difficulty.
  • You will be given 5 minutes to solve each problem.
  • During each 5 minute round, judges will come by to check your work.
  • The top competitors will advance each round.
  • Use jsFiddle.net in your favorite browser to mock up your solution. No IDE support.
  • No Google! No Bing! No cheating! Cheaters will be ridiculed publicly.
  • Compete for bragging rights and/or prizes.
